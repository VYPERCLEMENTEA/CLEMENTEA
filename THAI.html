THAI MILK TEA

CHARCOAL MILK TEA

Ingredients:

4 cups water

1/4 cup Thai tea mix (available at Asian markets or online)

1/2 cup sugar (adjust to taste)

1/2 cup evaporated milk

1/2 cup condensed milk (or more, to taste)

Ice cubes, for serving

PROCEDURE

Boil Water:

Bring the water to a boil in a large saucepan.

Steep Tea:

Add the Thai tea mix to the boiling water. Stir well,
then reduce heat and let simmer for about 3-5 minutes.

Sweeten:

Add sugar while the tea is hot, stirring until fully dissolved.
Adjust the sweetness according to your preference.

Adjust the sweetness according to your preference.

Use a tea strainer or a cheesecloth to strain the tea leaves from the mixture.
Dispose of the leaves.

Cool:

Allow the tea to cool to room temperature. You can speed up
this process by placing the tea in the refrigerator.

Prepare the Drink:

ill glasses with ice. Pour the cooled tea over the ice,
leaving enough room at the top for the milk.

Add Milk:

op each glass with a mix of condensed milk and evaporated milk, which will slowly sink
and swirl into the tea, creating a beautiful effect.

Serve:

Stir the tea and milk together before
drinking to enjoy the full flavor.
