CHARCOAL MILK TEA

Ingredients:

1-2 teaspoons black tea leaves (or 1-2 tea bags)

1 cup water

1 cup milk (dairy or plant-based)

1 teaspoon activated charcoal powder (food grade)

Sweetener (sugar, honey, or syrup) to taste

PROCEDURE

Boil Water:

Bring the water to a boil in a saucepan.

Steep Tea:

Add the black tea leaves to the boiling water.
Reduce heat and let it simmer for about 3-5 minutes.

Mix Charcoal:

While the tea is steeping, mix the activated charcoal powder with
a small amount of water to create a paste. This helps to prevent clumps in your drink.

Combine:

After the tea has steeped, remove it from heat and strain out the leaves.
Mix the charcoal paste into the hot tea and stir well.

Add Milk:

eat the milk until warm but not boiling, then add it to your charcoal tea mixture.

Sweeten:

Add your chosen sweetener to taste and stir well.

Serve:

Enjoy your charcoal milk tea warm. For a cold version,
let the tea cool down and serve over ice.
