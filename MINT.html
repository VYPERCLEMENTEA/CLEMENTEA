MINT MILK TEA

Ingredients:

1 cup water

1-2 teaspoons black tea leaves (or 1-2 tea bags)

A handful of fresh mint leaves (or 1-2 teaspoons dried mint)

1 cup milk (dairy or plant-based)

Sugar or honey to taste

PROCEDURE

Boil Water:

Bring water to a boil in a saucepan.

Steep Tea and Mint:

Add the black tea leaves and fresh mint leaves to the boiling water.
Reduce the heat and let it simmer for about 3-5 minutes.
The longer you steep, the stronger the mint flavor.

Add Milk:

Pour in the milk and bring the mixture back to
a slight boil, then turn off the heat.

Sweeten:

Add sugar or honey according to your taste
preference and stir until dissolved.

Strain and Serve:

Strain the tea into a cup to remove the leaves.
Serve hot for a cozy beverage or chill it for a refreshing iced tea.
