CARAMEL MILK TEA

Ingredients:

1 cup water

1-2 teaspoons black tea leaves (or 1-2 tea bags)

1 cup milk (dairy or plant-based)

2-3 tablespoons caramel sauce, plus extra for drizzling

Sugar to taste (optional, depending on
the sweetness of your caramel sauce)

PROCEDURE

Boil Water and Brew Tea:

Bring the water to a boil in a saucepan. Add the
black tea leaves or bags and steep for about 3-5
minutes, depending on how strong you like your tea.

Warm Milk:

In a separate saucepan, warm the milk
over medium heat but do not boil.

Add Caramel:

Stir the caramel sauce into the warm milk until well combined.
If you like your tea sweeter, you can add some sugar at this point.

Combine Tea and Milk:

Remove the tea leaves or bags from the water and
pour the caramel milk mixture into the brewed tea.
Stir well to combine everything evenly.

Serve:

Pour the caramel milk tea into a cup. Drizzle some
additional caramel sauce on top for garnish if desired.

Enjoy:

Serve your caramel milk tea hot.For a refreshing
twist, you can cool it down and serve it over ice.
