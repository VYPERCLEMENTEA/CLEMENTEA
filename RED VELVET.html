RED VELVET MILK TEA

Ingredients:

1 cup water

1-2 teaspoons black tea leaves (or 1-2 tea bags)

1 tablespoon cocoa powder

1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract

Red food coloring (a few drops, optional for color)

1 cup milk (dairy or plant-based)

1-2 tablespoons sugar or to taste

Whipped cream and a sprinkle of red velvet
cake crumbs (optional, for garnish)

PROCEDURE

Boil Water and Brew Tea:

Bring water to a boil in a saucepan. Add the black
tea leaves and let them steep for about 3-5 minutes.

Mix Cocoa:

In a small bowl, mix the cocoa powder with
a little hot water to make a smooth paste.

Combine Tea and Cocoa:

Add the cocoa paste and vanilla extract to the brewed tea.
Mix well. If using, add a few drops of red food coloring
to achieve the desired red velvet color.

Add Milk and Sweeten:

Add the milk to the saucepan and heat
the mixture until itâ€™s warm but not boiling.
Sweeten with sugar to your taste.

Serve:

Pour the tea into a cup. Optionally,
top with whipped cream and sprinkle with red velvet
cake crumbs for an extra special touch

Enjoy:

Serve your red velvet milk tea warm
or over ice for a refreshing treat.
