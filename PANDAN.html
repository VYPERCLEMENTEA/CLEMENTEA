PANDAN MILK TEA

Ingredients:

1 cup water

1-2 teaspoons black tea leaves (or 1-2 tea bags)

3-4 fresh pandan leaves, tied into a knot
(or 1/2 teaspoon pandan extract)

1 cup milk (dairy or plant-based)

Sugar to taste

PROCEDURE

Boil Water and Pandan:

Bring the water to a boil in a saucepan. Add the knotted pandan
leaves to the boiling water and let them simmer for about 5 minutes to infuse
the water with pandan flavor. If using pandan extract, add it after the water has boiled.

Steep Tea:

Add the black tea leaves or bags to the pandan-infused water.
Reduce the heat and let it simmer for about 3-5 minutes.

Remove Pandan and Tea Leaves

Take out the pandan leaves and tea leaves or bags.

Add Milk and Sweeten:

Add the milk and sugar to the pandan tea. Heat
the mixture until it's warm but not boiling,
stirring to dissolve the sugar.

Serve:

Pour the pandan milk tea into a cup and enjoy it warm.
Alternatively, you can cool it down and serve it
over ice for a refreshing drink.
