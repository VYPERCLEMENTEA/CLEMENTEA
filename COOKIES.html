COOKIES AND CREAM MILK TEA

Ingredients:

1 cup water

1-2 teaspoons black tea leaves (or 1-2 tea bags)

1 cup milk (dairy or plant-based)

2-3 tablespoons cookies and cream syrup
(available at specialty stores or online)

Crushed cookies (Oreo or similar),
for topping

Whipped cream, for garnish (optional)

PROCEDURE

Brew Tea:

Bring the water to a boil in a saucepan.
Add the black tea leaves or bags and let them steep for
about 3-5minutes, then remove the tea leaves or bags.

Warm Milk:

In another saucepan, gently heat the
milk until warm but not boiling.

Mix Tea and Milk:

Combine the brewed tea with the
warm milk in a large cup or pitcher.

Add Syrup:

Stir in the cookies and cream syrup. Adjust the amount
depending on how sweet and strong you like the flavor.

Serve:

Pour the milk tea into a serving glass.Top with
whipped cream and sprinkle crushed cookies over it.

Enjoy:

Serve immediately for a delicious,
dessert-like drink.
